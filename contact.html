Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and engagement process.

How long does it take to design and develop a website? expand_more
On average, a standard business website takes 10–20 days based on design complexity and content availability.
Websites with features like eCommerce, CRM, bookings, or custom integrations may require 2–6 weeks.
What do I need to provide before the project starts? expand_more
We only need a few basic things:
✔ Company details & services
✔ Logo (optional — we can design if needed)
✔ Photos & videos (optional — we can also handle)
✔ Preferred color theme/reference website (if any)
We handle the rest — design, structure, content writing, & launch.
Will my website be mobile-friendly? expand_more
Yes. All websites we build are 100% responsive — they work smoothly on mobile, tablet, and desktop with equal performance and UI consistency.
Can I update my website later by myself? expand_more
Absolutely. We develop websites on platforms where you can update text, images, blogs, and services without coding knowledge.
If preferred, we also offer maintenance and monthly site management.
Will my website rank on Google? expand_more
Every website we build includes SEO-friendly structure, metadata, optimized content, and fast loading for strong Google performance.
For faster ranking, we also provide full SEO and Google Ads services.
